UT Reporter — Support

Internal field-reporting app for Unlimited Technology / UT Global personnel.

Getting help

UT Reporter is a private, internal application. For help signing in, submitting a report, or reporting a problem with the app, contact the UT support team:

Email: utsupport@utglobal.com

Before you reach out

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  • Your name and the device you're using (e.g. iPhone 16, iPad).
  • The iOS/iPadOS version (Settings → General → About → Software Version).
  • Which report you were working with (Tailboard, Daily Status, Vehicle Safety Inspection, or Safety Report).
  • A short description of what happened and any on-screen error message.

Access

UT Reporter requires a valid Unlimited Technology organizational (Microsoft Entra) account and is distributed to company devices through your organization. If you cannot sign in, contact your IT administrator or the support address above.

See also our Privacy Policy.
