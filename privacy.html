UT Reporter — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

UT Reporter (the “App”) is a private, internal application provided by Unlimited Technology / UT Global (the “Company”) for use by its authorized employees and contractors. The App is used to complete and submit workplace safety and operational reports from an iPhone or iPad. This policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used. It is not a public consumer application and is not available for download by the general public.

Who this applies to

The App is intended solely for authorized Company personnel who sign in with a Company-issued Microsoft Entra ID (Azure Active Directory) account. It is not directed to the general public or to children.

Information the App handles

  • Account and identity information. When you sign in, the App authenticates you through Microsoft Entra ID and uses your name, email address, and user principal name to identify you and to associate the reports you submit with your account.
  • Report content you enter. Information you provide in a report — for example job and site details, vehicle inspection results, safety observations, incident descriptions, completion times, signatures, and any photos you attach.
  • Coworker directory look-ups. When a report requires you to select colleagues (for example a lead employee, general manager, project managers, or additional crew members), the App looks up names and email addresses from your organization's directory so you can choose them.
  • Device permissions. With your permission, the App uses the camera and photo library to let you attach jobsite and vehicle photos to a report. Photos are used only for the report you attach them to.
  • App preferences. A small display preference (light/dark appearance) is stored locally on your device only.

How the information is used

  • To let you create, edit, and submit workplace reports.
  • To store submitted reports in the Company's own Microsoft 365 / SharePoint environment.
  • To generate PDF copies of reports and, where enabled by the Company, to email completed reports to the Company-designated recipients.
  • To scope the reports you see to those associated with your own account.

The App does not use your information for advertising, and it does not track you across other apps or websites.

Where information is stored

Report data and attachments are stored in the Company's own Microsoft 365 / SharePoint Online tenant. Microsoft processes and hosts this data on the Company's behalf as its cloud service provider. Handling of data within that environment is governed by the Company's own information-management and Microsoft 365 policies.

Authentication and security

  • Sign-in uses the Microsoft Authentication Library (MSAL). The App never sees or stores your password.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device and are used to communicate with Microsoft services.
  • All communication with Microsoft Graph and SharePoint uses encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections.

Data sharing

The Company does not sell your information and does not share it with third parties for their own marketing. Information is shared only:

  • within the Company, as part of normal report review and record-keeping;
  • with Microsoft, as the Company's cloud service provider hosting Microsoft 365 / SharePoint; and
  • where required by law.

Data retention

Reports and related records are retained in the Company's Microsoft 365 environment in accordance with the Company's data-retention policies.

Your choices and access

Because the App is provided by your employer for work purposes, requests to access, correct, or delete report data should be directed to the Company (see contact below). Your use of the App is also subject to any applicable Company acceptable-use and privacy policies.

Tracking

The App does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies, and does not request permission to track you.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
